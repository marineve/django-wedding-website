Transportation

Taxis

Taxis are available round the clock. The rate for most taxi companies should be the same since they are regulated by the city of Victoria. Uptown Taxi can take up to 6 people, all others can take 4-5.

From the Airport or the BC Ferry's Terminal

Get on to Highway 17 towards Victoria. Highway 17 will become Blanshard St. Continue on Blanshard St. as it will become Belleville St. Past the BC Legislature building, the road will turn to the left, then to the right and after a park on your right, turn right onto Montreal St. and follow it to the end.

Directions from Douglas St.

Follow Douglas St. south through downtown Victoria until reach Belleville St. Turn right on to Belleville St. Past the BC Legislature building, the road will turn to the left, then to the right and after a park on your right, turn right onto Montreal St. and follow it to the end.

Parking
For those staying at the Inn, there is $17/night parking available.
For those that do not want to park at the Inn, there is public parking nearby. The corner of Quebec St. and Montreal St. is a 1 minute walk away has parking at $2/hour or $8/day.
