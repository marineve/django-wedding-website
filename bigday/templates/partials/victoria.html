Victoria

Getting There and Away

By Air

Victoria International Airport (YYJ)
Fly using your favourite airline into Victoria International Airport (YYJ). Then drive 30 minutes into Victoria or take the YYJ Airport Shuttle or a taxi to your hotel.

Driving directions: When leaving the Airport, take the 1st exit onto Willingdon Rd, at the second roundabout, take the 2nd exit, staying on Willingdon Rd. Continue onto Canora Rd and at the 2nd exit, merge onto the Patricia Bay Hwy/BC-17 ramp to Victoria/British Columbia 17 South. Follow Highway 17 into Victoria.

Harbour Air
Take a 25 minute flight over the beautiful Straight of Georgia and the Southern Gulf Islands on a float plane from Richmond, downtown Vancouver, Pitt Meadows, or Whistler. You will float by the Inn at Laurel Point as you arrive in Victoria’s Inner Harbour. Disembark a 15 minute walk or 3 minute drive around the Inner Harbour from the Inn

By Water

BC Ferries
A scenic 90-minute cruise through coastal BC waters. Board at the Tsawwassen terminal, 30-minutes south of Vancouver, and arrive in Swartz Bay in Sidney BC, 35-minute drive to Victoria. You can onto this ferry if you want to bring a vehicle or take the bus directly from the ferry terminal to downtown for $2.50 ($5.00 for a day pass). Taxis are also available.

Driving directions: You will exit the ferry directly onto Highway 17. Follow Highway 17 into Victoria.

V2V Vacations
Walk on this ferry from Downtown Vancouver, for a 3-hour and 15-minute ride to Victoria’s Inner Harbour. You CANNOT take a vehicle on this ferry.

Accommodations

The Inn at Laurel Point The Inn at Laurel Point is beautiful and has rooms available for those attending the wedding. Rooms start at $311.50/night ($280.35 for CAA members). A select 20 rooms are available from $214/night limited to the night before, night of, and night after the wedding.
Coast Victoria Hotel The Coast Victoria Hotel overlooks the outer harbour and is a 3 minute walk away from the Inn at Laurel point. Rooms start at $296.65/night.
Best Western Plus Inner Harbour The Best Western Plus Inner Harbour is a 5 minute walk from the Inn at Laurel point. Rooms start at $262/night or 235.80/night for CAA members.
Huntingdon Manor Hotel The Huntingdon Manor Hotel is a 5 minute walk from the Inn at Laurel point. Rooms start at $210/night.
Strathcona Hotel The Strathcona Hotel is a 15 minute walk or 5 minute drive from the Inn at Laurel point. Rooms start at $184/night or $165/night for CAA members.
Paul's Motor Inn Paul's Motor Inn is a 8 minute drive from the Inn at Laurel point. Rooms start at $154/night.
